Tako Jump

タコがジャンプで月を目指すアクションゲーム

How to Play

  • Jump button: Hold to charge, release to jump
  • Arrow buttons: Choose jump direction
  • Reach the moon to clear each stage
  • Avoid the rising water and eels
  • Collect jellyfish for air jumps
